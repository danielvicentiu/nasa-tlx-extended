# CWDP — Cognitive Workload Diagnostic Protocol

**NASA-TLX-9 Extended: measuring cognitive workload in AI-mediated work**

An open instrument extending the NASA Task Load Index (Hart & Staveland, 1988) with three dimensions for workplaces where AI tools are present — whether formally deployed or informally adopted ("Shadow AI").

## The three new dimensions

- **AI Trust** — calibration of reliance on AI output (EU AI Act Art. 26(7))
- **Override Anxiety** — stress of contradicting AI recommendations (EU AI Act Art. 14)
- **Concealment Load** — cognitive cost of hiding AI use (Directive 89/391/EEC + NIS2)

The phenomenon is documented globally (KPMG 2025: 57%; WalkMe 2025: 49%; Ivanti 2025: 32%). No prior instrument measures its *cognitive cost* as occupational workload. CWDP does.
